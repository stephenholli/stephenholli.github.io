Drink

Wineries & Breweries

The Hochatown area punches above its weight for wine, beer, and spirits.

Girls Gone Wine

Boutique Winery

Two locations (Broken Bow + Hochatown). Complimentary tastings at both (~15 min). VIP “G-Spot” seated tasting at Hochatown — ~$30-33/person, velvet couches. Reserve online or call 580-306-WINE.

Create custom-label wine — great birthday activity

Knotted Rope Winery

Winery

Reds and whites, sweet to dry. 83 N Lukfata Trail.

Sun-Thu 12-5pm · Fri-Sat 12-7pm

Mountain Fork Brewery

Brewery + Restaurant

20 craft beers on tap with brick-oven pizza. Tours & tastings available. 89 N Lukfata Trail.

Hochatown Distilling Co.

Bourbon Distillery

First Bottled in Bond bourbon made in Oklahoma. Tours and tastings. 41 N Lukfata Trail.

🍷

Wine Tour Idea

Hit Girls Gone Wine (Hochatown, on Hwy 259) then walk the Lukfata Trail cluster — Knotted Rope, Mountain Fork Brewery, and Hochatown Distilling are all within steps of each other. Easy afternoon, no driving between stops.

Beavers Bend State Park

~10-15 min from cabin · 12+ miles of trails · River access · Nature center

🥾 Hiking

Cedar Bluff Nature Trail (1 mi, moderate) — great Mountain Fork River views. David Boren Trail is the main connected system.

🐎 Horseback Riding

Beavers Bend Depot & Trail Rides — guided 1-hour, 2.5 mi flat trail. Kids 5+. Closed Thursdays.

$50/person

🏞 Scenic Overlooks

Skyline Trail and Lookout Mountain Trail offer panoramic forest views.

🦜 Nature Center

Aquariums, taxidermy exhibits, naturalist-led programs.
